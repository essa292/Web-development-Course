Babar Azam

Babar Azam, born on October 15, 1994, in Lahore, is one of Pakistan’s finest cricketers and among the world’s top batsmen. Known for his elegant batting style and remarkable consistency, he has broken several records across formats. Babar became the fastest player to reach 1,000, 2,000, 3,000, 4,000, and 5,000 ODI runs for Pakistan and has also been ranked the No. 1 ODI batsman in the ICC rankings multiple times. He holds the record for being the fastest Asian to score 10 ODI centuries and is the only Pakistani captain to score centuries in all three formats of the game. With over 12,000 international runs and more than 30 centuries, his statistics reflect his dominance in modern cricket. Under his leadership, Pakistan reached the 2022 T20 World Cup final. Fans across the globe admire Babar for his calm temperament, graceful cover drives, and unwavering dedication to the sport.
